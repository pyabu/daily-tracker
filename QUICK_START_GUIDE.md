## How to Use

### 1. Open the App
Simply open `index.html` in your browser or visit your hosted URL.

### 2. First Time Setup
- Enter your name and age in the welcome screen
- This personalizes your experience

### 3. Add Your First Entry
1. Click "Add Entry" in the sidebar
2. Select meal type (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Dinner, Snack)
3. Enter blood sugar level (or check "Not Taken")
4. Enter insulin dose (or check "Not Taken")
5. Add optional notes
6. Click "Save Entry"

### 4. Check Your Goals
1. Click "Goals" in the sidebar
2. See your daily progress ring
3. Track your weekly streak
4. View unlocked achievements

### 5. View Your Data
- **Dashboard**: See today's stats and timeline
- **History**: View 7-day trend chart and all entries
- **Insights**: Get personalized health insights
- **Diet Guide**: Browse food recommendations

### 6. Configure Settings
- **Profile**: Update name and age
- **WhatsApp**: Set up auto-send reports
- **Reminders**: Enable daily reminders
- **Contacts**: Add emergency contacts
- **Alerts**: Configure automatic alerts
- **Export**: Download data as Excel or CSV

## Tips for Best Experience

### Daily Routine
1. **Morning**: Log your morning reading
2. **Throughout Day**: Log after each meal
3. **Evening**: Check your Goals page progress
4. **Night**: Review your daily report

### Streak Building
- Log at least one entry every day
- Check the Goals page to see your streak
- Aim for 7 days to unlock the streak badge!

### Health Monitoring
- Keep readings in 70-140 mg/dL range
- Add notes about what you ate
- Review Insights page weekly
- Export data monthly for doctor visits

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See Changes?
1. Hard refresh: `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Close and reopen the browser

### Mobile Menu Not Working?
- Tap the hamburger icon (☰) in the top-left
- Tap outside the menu to close it

### Goals Not Updating?
- Add more entries to see progress
- Refresh the page
- Check that entries have valid sugar levels

### Firebase Not Syncing?
- Check internet connection
- Look for sync status in sidebar footer
- Check browser console for errors
