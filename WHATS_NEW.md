### 🆕 New Features (LIVE NOW!)

#### 1. **Goals Page** 🎯
**What it does**: Track your daily goals and progress

**Features**:
- **Circular Progress Ring**: Visual progress indicator
- **Daily Goal**: Keep blood sugar in range (70-140 mg/dL)
- **Progress Percentage**: See your completion rate
- **Statistics**: X/5 readings in range today

**How to access**: Click "Goals" in sidebar

#### 2. **Weekly Streak Calendar** 🔥
**What it does**: Track consecutive days of logging

**Features**:
- 7-day calendar view
- Completed days highlighted in green
- Today marked with special border
- Streak counter with fire emoji
- Motivational messages

**Benefits**:
- Build healthy habits
- Visual motivation
- Track consistency
- Celebrate streaks

#### 3. **Achievement System** 🏆
**What it does**: Unlock badges for milestones

**Achievements**:
- 🥇 **First Entry**: Log your first entry
- 📅 **7-Day Streak**: Log entries for 7 consecutive days
- 🎯 **Perfect Day**: All readings in target range
- 💯 **100 Entries**: Log 100 total entries

**Features**:
- Locked/unlocked states
- Visual feedback when unlocked
- Gamification element
- Increased motivation

## 📱 How to See the Changes

### Option 1: Refresh Browser
1. Open your app in browser
2. Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
3. Hard refresh to clear cache
4. See the new purple theme!

### Option 2: Clear Cache
1. Open browser settings
2. Clear cache and cookies
3. Reload the page
4. Enjoy the new look!

### Option 3: Incognito Mode
1. Open incognito/private window
2. Navigate to your app
3. See changes immediately
